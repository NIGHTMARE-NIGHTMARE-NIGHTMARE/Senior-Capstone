iBIS: Interactive Bot for In-Depth Study

Say hello to the Interactive Bot for In-depth Study, or iBIS!

With iBIS, we want to provide a tool for all levels of coders. Built with the detailed Rasa AI framework, and connected with the leading back-end web framework of Django, iBIS is designed to provide an in-depth and interactive learning environment tailored to each users needs. By providing information on each algorithm, challenges, as well as pseudocode, iBIS is designed for the beginner coders, trying to learn each algorithm, as well as a intermediate and coding masters, who are looking to refresh their skills.
